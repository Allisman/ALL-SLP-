Areas of Support

What I help with

Every student is unique. Here are the main areas where I provide therapy support:

Social Communication

Building skills in peer interaction, conversation, perspective-taking, and friendship-building for navigating social situations with confidence.

Academic Language

Supporting language for learning — including vocabulary, following directions, comprehension, and expressing ideas clearly in classroom settings.

Literacy

Strengthening reading and writing skills, including phonological awareness, decoding, fluency, and written expression.

Language Processing

Helping students understand language better — working with auditory processing, following multi-step directions, and organizing thoughts.

Expressive Language

Building confidence in expressing thoughts, ideas, and feelings — from word choice to sentence structure to storytelling.

Speech Sound Disorders

Supporting clear speech through targeted practice and strategies for specific sound patterns or articulation concerns.
